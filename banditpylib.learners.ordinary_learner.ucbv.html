UCBV policy [AMSzepesvari09]

At time \(t\), play arm

\[\mathrm{argmax}_{i \in [0, N-1]} \left\{ \bar{\mu}_i(t) + \sqrt{ \frac{ 2 \bar{V}_i(t) \ln(t) }{T_i(t)} }+ \frac{ b \ln(t) }{T_i(t)} \right\}\]

Note

Reward has to be bounded within \([0, b]\).
